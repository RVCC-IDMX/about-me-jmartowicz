Goals

To work at completing my associates degree in Information Systems to eventually transfer to a bachelors program. This includes getting more proficient in the many subjects and skills that encompass the field, as well as pursuing certain certifications which will make me more competitive in the workplace.

I want to be...

good at what I do and be successful from working hard in IT. I want to know what I am doing and be proficient in my career. To get anywhere in life, I believe you have to be content in suffering and pain to achieve any goals, and the closer I get to mastering following the most rational path in life rather than succombing to impulse or other vices, the more successful I will be. Oftentimes,I feel like you already know what has to be done, but your impulses and emotions rarely allow yourself to see it through and commit on the best branch in life.

Some things that I am up to are..

pulling myself out of COVID lockdown, working on my car, perfecting myself. Now that we are at the end of the semester, I realize I have to set short term goals, and complete them one at a time, to eventually achieve long term goals, as I feel I will be more effective that way. It is very easy to get overwhelmed by large projects, but once you realize they can just be a bunch of small projects, it seems alot easier to complete it. So, instead of vague, foggy things to be up to, I will try and be more focused and calculated in my daily activities.
